Hungary contends with environmental challenges, including air pollution, water contamination, and biodiversity loss. Urban areas experience air quality issues due to industrial emissions and traffic. Agricultural runoff contributes to water pollution, affecting rivers and lakes. Habitat destruction and fragmentation threaten biodiversity, while invasive species further impact ecosystems. Hungary also faces challenges in waste management and recycling. Addressing these eco problems requires sustainable practices, stricter environmental regulations, and conservation efforts to protect Hungary's natural resources and biodiversity.
