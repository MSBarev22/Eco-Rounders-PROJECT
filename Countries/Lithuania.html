Lithuania contends with environmental challenges, including air pollution, soil degradation, and waste management issues. Urban areas, especially industrial zones, contribute to air pollution, affecting public health. Agriculture and industrial activities contribute to soil degradation, impacting agricultural productivity. Waste management challenges include issues with recycling and landfill management. Lithuania is actively working on measures to improve air quality, promote sustainable agriculture practices, and enhance waste management to address these eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability. Additionally, the country focuses on renewable energy and biodiversity conservation efforts.
