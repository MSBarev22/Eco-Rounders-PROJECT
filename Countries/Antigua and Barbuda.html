Antigua and Barbuda confront environmental challenges such as coastal erosion from rising sea levels and climate change, along with heightened vulnerability to hurricanes, jeopardizing their coastlines, communities, and infrastructure. Additionally, issues related to waste management and biodiversity conservation require attention for sustainable development.
Antigua and Barbuda is an independent Commonwealth country in the Caribbean region, comprised of two main islands, Antigua and Barbuda. St. John's, situated on Antigua, serves as the capital.
