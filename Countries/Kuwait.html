Kuwait faces environmental challenges, including desertification, water scarcity, and air pollution. The arid climate contributes to desertification, impacting soil quality and vegetation. Water scarcity is a critical issue, with limited freshwater resources and high dependence on desalination. Air pollution, especially in urban areas, results from industrial emissions and vehicular traffic. Kuwait is actively working on sustainable initiatives, such as afforestation projects, water conservation measures, and efforts to improve air quality, to address these eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability.
