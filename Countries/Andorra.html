Andorra faces specific ecological challenges including deforestation, soil erosion from ski resort development, habitat fragmentation, and water pollution due to urbanization and tourism. Its small size exacerbates these issues, demanding sustainable land use and conservation efforts for its unique mountainous ecosystems and scarce water resources.
Andorra, a small landlocked principality in the Pyrenees Mountains between France and Spain in Europe, has Andorra la Vella as its capital, with an estimated population of around 77,000 people.
