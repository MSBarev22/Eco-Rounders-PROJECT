Kyrgyzstan faces environmental challenges, including deforestation, water pollution, and soil degradation. Unsustainable logging practices contribute to deforestation, impacting ecosystems and biodiversity. Water pollution results from industrial discharges and inadequate waste management. Agricultural activities and overgrazing contribute to soil degradation, affecting arable land. Kyrgyzstan is working on sustainable forestry practices, improved waste management, and soil conservation initiatives to address these eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability.
