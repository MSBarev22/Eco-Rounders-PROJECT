Luxembourg, being a relatively small and affluent European nation, faces environmental challenges associated with urbanization and transportation. Issues include air pollution in urban areas, primarily due to traffic emissions, and the management of waste generated by a prosperous and dense population. The country is actively working on sustainable practices, waste management strategies, and green initiatives to mitigate these challenges and enhance its environmental sustainability. Luxembourg's affluence and commitment to sustainability contribute to ongoing efforts to address its environmental impact.
