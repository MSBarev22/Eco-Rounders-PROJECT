Italy confronts various environmental challenges, including air pollution, water quality issues, and biodiversity loss. Urban areas, particularly major cities, experience air pollution from industrial activities and vehicular emissions. Agricultural runoff and inadequate waste management contribute to water pollution in certain regions. Biodiversity faces threats from habitat loss, climate change, and invasive species. Italy is actively working on sustainable initiatives, stricter environmental regulations, and conservation efforts to address these eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability.
