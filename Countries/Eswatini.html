Eswatini faces environmental challenges, including deforestation, soil erosion, and water scarcity. Rampant logging and agricultural expansion contribute to deforestation, disrupting ecosystems and threatening biodiversity. Soil erosion intensifies due to unsustainable farming practices, compromising agricultural productivity and exacerbating downstream impacts. Water scarcity is a pressing issue, aggravated by climate change and increased demand, leading to challenges in meeting the needs of both people and ecosystems. Addressing these eco problems requires sustainable land management practices, conservation efforts, and effective water resource management in Eswatini.
