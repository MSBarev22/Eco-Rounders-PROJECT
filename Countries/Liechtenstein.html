Liechtenstein, a small European principality, faces relatively limited environmental challenges compared to larger nations. However, it deals with issues such as air pollution, mainly from traffic and industry, and the impacts of climate change. Being landlocked, the country also focuses on water quality and resource conservation. Liechtenstein is proactive in implementing sustainable practices, renewable energy initiatives, and environmental policies to address these challenges and enhance its environmental sustainability.
