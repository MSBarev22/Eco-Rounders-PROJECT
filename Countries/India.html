Top Ecological Countries
India faces a range of environmental challenges, including air pollution, water scarcity, deforestation, and biodiversity loss. Rapid industrialization and urbanization contribute to severe air pollution in major cities. Agricultural runoff and industrial discharges impact water quality, leading to water scarcity in certain regions. Deforestation, driven by urban expansion and logging, threatens biodiversity and ecosystems. Climate change adds to these challenges, with increased frequency of extreme weather events. India is working towards sustainable development, emphasizing renewable energy, afforestation, and water conservation to address these complex eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability.
