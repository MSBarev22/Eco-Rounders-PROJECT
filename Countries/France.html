France grapples with various environmental challenges, including air pollution in urban areas, water pollution from agricultural runoff, and biodiversity loss due to habitat destruction. Pesticide use in agriculture raises concerns for both ecosystems and human health. Additionally, France faces climate change impacts, evidenced by more frequent heatwaves and extreme weather events. The reliance on nuclear power for energy, while reducing carbon emissions, poses challenges regarding nuclear waste disposal. To address these eco problems, France is working on transitioning to renewable energy sources, implementing stricter environmental regulations, and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.
