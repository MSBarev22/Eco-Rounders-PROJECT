Albania faces ecological challenges including deforestation, soil degradation, water pollution and scarcity, air pollution mainly in urban areas, and insufficient waste management infrastructure, leading to environmental pollution.
Albania is a country in Southeastern Europe, situated on the Balkan Peninsula. It is bordered by Montenegro to the northwest, Kosovo to the northeast, North Macedonia to the east, and Greece to the south. It has a western coastline along the Adriatic Sea and a southern coastline along the Ionian Sea.
