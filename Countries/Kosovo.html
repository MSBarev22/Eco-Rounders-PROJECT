Kosovo confronts environmental challenges, including air pollution, deforestation, and inadequate waste management. Heavy reliance on coal for energy production contributes to severe air pollution, impacting public health. Deforestation, driven by logging and land-use changes, threatens ecosystems and biodiversity. Inefficient waste management practices contribute to pollution and environmental degradation. Kosovo is working on improving air quality, promoting sustainable forestry practices, and implementing better waste management strategies to address these eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability.
