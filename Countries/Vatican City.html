Vatican City, an independent city-state enclaved within Rome, Italy, is the smallest sovereign state in the world. It is the spiritual and administrative center of the Roman Catholic Church. Home to St. Peter's Basilica and the Vatican Museums, the city-state is rich in religious and artistic treasures, including the Sistine Chapel. Governed by the Pope, Vatican City is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a significant pilgrimage destination, attracting visitors with its religious significance and cultural heritage.
