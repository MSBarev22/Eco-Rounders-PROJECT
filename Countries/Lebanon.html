Lebanon confronts environmental challenges, including deforestation, water pollution, and waste management issues. Rampant logging contributes to deforestation, disrupting ecosystems and threatening biodiversity. Water pollution results from industrial discharges and inadequate waste management, impacting rivers and coastal areas. Lebanon's waste management crisis poses threats to public health and the environment. Additionally, the country faces air pollution, especially in urban areas. Lebanon is actively working on reforestation efforts, improved waste management, and water quality initiatives to address these eco problems and enhance environmental sustainability.
