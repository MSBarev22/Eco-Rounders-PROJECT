Top Ecological Countries
Gabon faces notable environmental challenges, including deforestation, wildlife poaching, and oil-related environmental impacts. The country's rich biodiversity is threatened by deforestation driven by logging and agricultural expansion, compromising crucial ecosystems. Wildlife, including iconic species like elephants and gorillas, is at risk due to rampant poaching. The oil industry, a significant economic contributor, poses environmental challenges such as habitat disruption, oil spills, and pollution. Sustainable resource management, conservation efforts, and responsible oil industry practices are crucial for addressing these eco problems in Gabon and preserving its unique natural heritage.
