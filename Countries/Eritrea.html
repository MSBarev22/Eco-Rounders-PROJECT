Top Ecological Countries
Eritrea, located in the Horn of Africa, grapples with a range of environmental challenges that impact its ecosystems and pose risks to its population. Deforestation, largely driven by agricultural expansion, logging, and fuelwood collection, leads to soil erosion, habitat loss, and reduced biodiversity. The country's semi-arid climate and susceptibility to drought exacerbate concerns about land degradation and food security.
Eritrea faces environmental challenges including deforestation from agricultural expansion and logging, leading to habitat loss and biodiversity reduction. The country experiences water scarcity due to erratic rainfall and over-extraction of groundwater, impacting both agriculture and communities.
