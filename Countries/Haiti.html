Top Ecological Countries
Haiti faces severe environmental challenges, including deforestation, soil erosion, and water pollution. Rampant logging for fuel and construction, coupled with unsustainable agricultural practices, has led to extensive deforestation, disrupting ecosystems and exacerbating soil erosion. Poor waste management and inadequate sanitation contribute to water pollution, posing risks to public health. Haiti's vulnerability to natural disasters, particularly hurricanes, further compounds these issues. Sustainable reforestation, improved agricultural practices, and effective waste management are crucial for addressing these eco problems and promoting environmental resilience in Haiti.
