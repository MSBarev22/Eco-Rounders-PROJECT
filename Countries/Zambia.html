ECO INFORMATION ABOUT THE COUNTRY
Zambia, located in southern Africa, is known for its diverse landscapes, wildlife, and the majestic Victoria Falls. Lusaka, the capital, combines modernity with a relaxed atmosphere. The country is home to national parks like South Luangwa and Lower Zambezi, offering opportunities for safaris and adventure activities. Zambia's portion of Victoria Falls, one of the world's largest waterfalls, is a UNESCO World Heritage site. The country's cultural diversity is expressed through traditional ceremonies, dance, and crafts. With its natural beauty and warm hospitality, Zambia is a destination for those seeking an authentic African experience.
